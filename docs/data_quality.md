Data Quality & Assumptions

Data Source:

All datasets used in this project are synthetically generated for demonstration purposes.
No real student data has been used.

Retention Definition:

Retention is defined as: Re-enrolment anywhere within the institution in the following academic year.

(This definition does not represent graduation or completion metrics.)

Risk Model Limitations:

- The success score is illustrative and not statistically optimised.
- Risk thresholds are heuristic.
- No regression or machine learning techniques were applied.
- The model is designed for interpretability rather than predictive accuracy.

Scope Limitations:
- Focus limited to Year 1 entrants.
- Five academic years included.
- Real-time data ingestion and regulatory reporting not implemented.
